...What's that? You don't understand the music? Well, a little tidbit for you!
The bands who played those songs (sans Nintendo) are actual bands from Japan. Yes, they're more or less speaking Japanese in these songs - it's distorted so much in a way that it sounds gibberish to some people. However, since these songs came from a game where ink squids have their own language, these songs were spoken in what we call "Inkling"... which is what we also call these magnificent ink squids.
But will we ever know what it means? Probably not. I'm just here for the music, man.
